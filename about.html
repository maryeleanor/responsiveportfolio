About Mary Eleanor

UX | UI | Design | Photography | Artist | Swimmer | Triathlete

Hi! I'm Mary. An artist, designer, and photographer in San Francisco CA.

My passions for art and philanthropy have contributed to my varied work history. I have experience working with art galleries, photography studios, non-profits, youth hostels, record labels, event production, retail and schools. Founder of D.R.E.A.M., and Art Against Cancer; I consciously seek and create work with meaning.

Taking after my mother and grandfather, I began photographing sunsets and printing in basement darkrooms at an early age. I went on to study Photography and Computer Science at Bennington College. Essentially, an artist-in-residency, culminating in photography printed on fabric and reactive digital installations. Shortly after graduating, seeking a more diverse environment, I headed west to San Francisco. I have most recently been assisting MFA students at The Academy of Art University and studying UX design at General Assembly. My years of creative experience range from assisting in technical studios to designing web architectures and mobile apps. Outside of work, you can often find me swimming in the bay, photographing the coast and training for triathlons.

Skills:   Research, Usability Testing, Personas, Story Boarding, Sitemaps, Information Architecture, User Flows, Process Flows, Wireframes, Prototypes, Human Centered Design Strategy, Systematic Thinking, Facilitation, Coordination, Collaboration, Creative Problem Solving, Customer Service, Management, Project Management, Administration

Tools:   Adobe Creative Cloud [Photoshop, Illustrator, Fireworks, Dreamweaver, InDesign, Lightroom] and Adobe Acrobat Pro, Sketch, Omnigraffle, Balsamiq, Invision, Axure, MS Office [Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Outlook], Keynote; Google Apps, Webcheckout, Zoho, Asana, Trello, Salesforce, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C, and Github.
